SNEAKY BIGFOOT

A cryptid's life in the great outdoors

Deep in Pine Hollow, a Sasquatch family lives hidden in a mossy den: Mama Bigfoot and your two little ones. You are Papa Bigfoot — gentle giant, devoted husband and dad, and very, very hungry.

The campers left their coolers unguarded. The ranger is napping (mostly). Sneak through the campground, swipe snacks, earn a few coins, stay out of sight — and bring dinner home to your little ones.

WASD / Arrows — Move
Shift — Sneak (quiet & low suspicion)
X — Sprint (burns energy)
E — Grab / Feed kids / Sell
F — Eat a snack yourself
Q — Toss on disguise (at the laundry line)
